# California Child Care Resource & Referral Directory - All 58 Counties

**Purpose:** Complete R&R agency contacts for every California county, with special guidance for rural and small counties.

**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

## Quick Reference by Region

### Urban Counties (Population 500K+)
These counties have multiple R&R options and typically shorter waitlists:
- **Los Angeles** - Multiple APPs by region
- **San Diego** - Centralized YMCA CRS
- **Orange** - Children's Home Society
- **Riverside** - RCOE Children's Services
- **San Bernardino** - Child Care Resource Center
- **Santa Clara** - Multiple options
- **Alameda** - 4Cs and BANANAS
- **Sacramento** - Child Action Inc.
- **Contra Costa** - CocoKids
- **Fresno** - Central Valley Children's Services Network
- **San Francisco** - Children's Council, Wu Yee

### Rural Counties (Population under 50K)
**Important:** Rural counties often have limited center-based options. Family child care and license-exempt providers are often the primary options. Consider neighboring larger counties for more choices.

## Statewide Resources

### When Your County Has Limited Options

1. **Call 211** - Available in every county for local resource connections
2. **MyChildCarePlan.org** - Online search tool with live support
3. **California R&R Network Hotline:** 1-800-KIDS-793 (1-800-543-7793)
4. **Consider neighboring counties** - Especially for rural areas

### For Very Rural Counties

If you're in Alpine, Modoc, Sierra, Trinity, Mono, Inyo, or other very rural counties:

- **Family Child Care** is often the most available option
- **License-Exempt Care** (family, friends, neighbors) may be necessary
- **TrustLine** background checks available for any provider: 1-800-822-8490
- **Consider providers in neighboring larger counties**
- **Regional Center services** still available regardless of location
