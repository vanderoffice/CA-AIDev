# San Diego County Childcare Guide

**Population:** ~3.3 million (2nd largest county in CA)
**Region:** 1 (Higher Cost of Living)
**Primary Agency:** San Diego County Health & Human Services Agency (HHSA)

---

## Overview

San Diego County is California's second-largest county by population, stretching from the coast to the desert border. The county has a well-organized childcare subsidy system with strong military family support due to the large military presence.

## County-Specific Features

### Military Family Support

San Diego has one of the largest military populations in the US:

| Base | Branch | Childcare Contact |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Naval Base San Diego | Navy | Base CDC |
| Marine Corps Base Camp Pendleton | Marines | MCCS Childcare |
| Naval Air Station North Island | Navy | Base CDC |
| Marine Corps Air Station Miramar | Marines | MCCS Childcare |

**Military Childcare Resources:**
- **MCCYN (Military Child Care in Your Neighborhood):** Subsidies for off-base care
- **Military OneSource:** (800) 342-9647
- **Child Care Aware Fee Assistance:** childcareaware.org

### Border Region Considerations

- Many families cross from Mexico for work
- Bilingual services widely available
- Some providers near border specialize in split schedules

### Centralized Eligibility List (CEL)

San Diego uses a countywide CEL managed by YMCA CRS:
- Single application for most subsidy programs
- Easier tracking than fragmented systems
- Apply once, get matched to available programs

## Application Process

### CalWORKs (Stages 1-3)

```
STAGE 1 (While on Cash Aid)
──────────────────────────
1. Apply for CalWORKs at HHSA
2. Meet with employment services
3. Request childcare on CW 101 form
4. Choose provider
5. HHSA authorizes Stage 1 care

STAGE 2/3 (After Cash Aid)
──────────────────────────
1. When leaving cash aid, HHSA refers to YMCA CRS
2. YMCA CRS contacts you within 2 weeks
3. Submit updated employment documentation
4. Continue with same or new provider
```

### Non-CalWORKs Subsidies

1. Call YMCA CRS: (800) 481-2151
2. Complete application
3. Get placed on Centralized Eligibility List
4. Wait for available slot
5. Choose from participating providers

## Tips for San Diego County

### Finding Providers

1. **Start with YMCA CRS:** (800) 481-2151 - they maintain the main provider database
2. **Military families:** Check base CDCs first (often subsidized)
3. **Near border:** Look for bilingual programs and flexible schedules
4. **Use MyChildCare.ca.gov:** State database for licensed providers

### Navigating the System

- **Centralized application:** One application covers most programs
- **Military priority:** Service members often get priority placement
- **Bilingual support:** Spanish widely available
- **Regional offices:** YMCA CRS has multiple locations for in-person help

### Quality Indicators

- Quality Preschool Initiative (QPI) rated programs
- NAEYC-accredited centers
- QRIS participation
