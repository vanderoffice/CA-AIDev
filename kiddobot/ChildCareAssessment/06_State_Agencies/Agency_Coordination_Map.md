## Shared Responsibilities

### Income Eligibility (CDSS Sets, Others Use)

| Program | Agency | Uses CDSS SMI Tables? |
|---------|--------|----------------------|
| CalWORKs childcare | CDSS | Yes (plus MBSAC/MAP) |
| APP/CCDF | CDSS | Yes (85% SMI) |
| CSPP | CDE | Yes (100% SMI) |
| Head Start | CDE | No (federal poverty level) |

### Provider Licensing (CDSS CCL Division)

All licensed providers—whether serving CDE or CDSS programs—are licensed by CDSS Community Care Licensing:

| Provider Type | License Required | Licensor |
|---------------|------------------|----------|
| Family Child Care | Yes | CDSS CCL |
| Child Care Center | Yes | CDSS CCL |
| Preschool (CSPP) | Yes | CDSS CCL |
| TK in public school | No (school operated) | CDE oversight |
| FFN (license-exempt) | No | N/A |

### Health Requirements (CDPH Sets, All Enforce)

Every licensed childcare setting must verify CDPH requirements:

| Requirement | Set by | Enforced by |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| Immunizations | CDPH | Childcare provider |
| Health exam | CDPH | Childcare provider |
| Outbreak protocols | CDPH | Provider + local health |
| TB screening | CDPH (county specific) | Provider |

## Recent Coordination Improvements

### Master Plan for Early Learning and Care

California has been working to better coordinate between CDSS and CDE:

| Initiative | Status | Goal |
|------------|--------|------|
| Mixed delivery system | In progress | Seamless access regardless of program |
| Unified income verification | Proposed | One application, multiple programs |
| Data system integration | In progress | Share enrollment data across agencies |
| Rate equalization | Ongoing | Similar pay for similar work |

### Department of Early Childhood (Proposed)

California has discussed creating a unified early childhood agency:
- Would consolidate CDSS and CDE early childhood functions
- Goal: Simplify system for families and providers
- Status: Under discussion, not yet implemented

## Navigating the System: Tips for Families

### Where to Start

1. **Call 211** — Connects you to all programs
2. **Local CCR&R** — [rrnetwork.org](https://rrnetwork.org) — Knows all local options
3. **School district** — For TK and CSPP
4. **County welfare** — For CalWORKs

### Common Confusion Points

| Confusion | Clarification |
|-----------|---------------|
| "CDSS rejected me but I heard I qualify for preschool" | CDSS programs (85% SMI) differ from CDE programs (100% SMI for CSPP) |
| "I applied to preschool but they're asking about childcare subsidies" | Some CSPP programs also contract with APPs; they may ask about both |
| "The school says my child isn't vaccinated but my doctor says they are" | Records may not be in CAIR; ask doctor to submit to registry |
| "I got a voucher but the preschool won't take it" | CSPP-only programs may not accept APP vouchers; ask about their funding source |

### Questions to Ask Any Program

1. Which agency funds this program? (CDSS, CDE, federal)
2. What are the income limits?
3. What hours are available?
4. Do you accept APP vouchers?
5. What immunizations does my child need?
