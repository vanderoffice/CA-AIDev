# California Restaurant Business License Requirements and Fees (2026)

**Research Date:** 2026-01-18
**Source:** Perplexity Deep Research with 60 citations

## Key 2025-2026 Legislative Changes

### AB 592 - Outdoor Dining Flexibility
- Permits windows, folding doors, non-fixed storefronts during operating hours
- No longer requires full enclosure at all times
- Signed October 2025 by Governor Newsom

### AB 671 - Expedited Permitting
- Creates voluntary expedited permitting for small restaurants
- Licensed architects/engineers can self-certify plans
- 20 business day approval deadline (auto-approved if missed)
- Reduces 3-12 month timelines to ~3-4 weeks

### ABC Fee Increases (Jan 1, 2026)
- All fees increased 2.72% (CPI adjustment)
- Applies to all license types

## Food Handler Certification

- **Requirement:** All food handlers within 30 days of hire
- **Cost:** As low as $7.95/employee (online ANAB-accredited)
- **Valid:** 3 years
- **SB 476 (Jan 1, 2024):** Employers must pay all training costs
- **Provider:** Premier Food Safety, ~90 minutes online

### Restaurant Beverage Program (LA)
- Streamlined local approval: ~4 weeks, ~$5,000
- Traditional CUP: 6-12 months, ~$13,000

## MEHKO (Home Kitchen) Alternative

- Up to 30 meals/day, 90 meals/week
- Annual revenue cap: $100,000
- Application fee: $597 (subsidized through June 2026)
- Annual permit: $347
- Requires Food Manager Certificate
