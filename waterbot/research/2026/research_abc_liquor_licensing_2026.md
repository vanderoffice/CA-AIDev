# California ABC Alcohol Beverage License Requirements (2026)

**Research Date:** 2026-01-18
**Source:** Perplexity Deep Research with 60 citations

## Type 41 vs Type 47 Comparison

| Feature | Type 41 (Beer & Wine) | Type 47 (Full Liquor) |
|---------|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Beverages** | Beer and wine only | Beer, wine, all spirits |
| **Food Requirement** | 50% gross sales from food | 50% gross sales from food |
| **Minors Allowed** | Yes | Yes |
| **Off-Premises** | Beer/wine in sealed containers | Beer/wine only (no spirits) |
| **Application Fee** | $905 | Priority: $19,840 / Non-priority: $1,135 |

## Additional Fees (2026)

- Appeals Board Surcharge: 3% of license fee
- CHP Surcharge: $10/year (all on-sale)
- Business Practices Surcharge: $24/year (Type 47 only)
- Transfer Fee (Person-to-Person): Type 41 = $420, Type 47 = $1,565

## Secondary Market Prices (Los Angeles County)

- **Type 47 typical range:** $85,000 - $100,000
- **Broker commission:** 5-6% (~$5,500-$6,500)
- **Total acquisition cost:** $95,000 - $115,000+

**Why so expensive?** Population-based limitation: 1 license per 2,000 persons per county

## 2025-2026 Legislative Changes

### SB 395 - San Francisco Hospitality Zone (Jan 1, 2026)
- Up to 20 new Type 47 licenses in downtown SF
- Max 10 first year, then 5/year
- **Anti-flipping provision:** Cannot sell above original state fee

### AB 828 - Los Angeles Type 87 Licenses (Jan 1, 2026)
- Up to 40 new neighborhood-restricted licenses in LA County
- Max 12/year
- Specific census tract restrictions

### AB 1008 - San Luis Obispo (Jan 1, 2026)
- Up to 10 additional Type 47 licenses
- Max 5/year

### AB 233 - Colusa County (Jan 1, 2026)
- Up to 10 additional Type 47 licenses

## LA Restaurant Beverage Program (Streamlined Permitting)

- **Timeline:** 6-10 weeks vs 6-12 months for traditional CUP
- **Cost:** ~$1,500-$3,000 (vs ~$13,000 for CUP)
- **Eligibility:** RBP-eligible areas only (check map)
- **Requirements:** 50+ operating standards compliance

**Contact:** planning.ccu@lacity.org
**URL:** https://planning.lacity.gov/restaurant-beverage-program

## Processing Times

- **Original application:** ~90 days
- **Person-to-person transfer:** ~75 days
- **30-day posting period required**
