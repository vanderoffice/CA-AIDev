# California Real Estate Licensing Requirements (2025-2026)

**Research Date:** 2026-01-18
**Source:** Perplexity Deep Research with 60 citations

## Salesperson License

### Requirements
- Age: 18+ years
- Education: 135 hours (3 courses × 45 hours each)
- Exam: 150 questions, 70% to pass
- Honesty/character requirements

### Required Courses (135 hours total)
1. **Real Estate Principles** (mandatory)
2. **Real Estate Practice** (mandatory) - must include implicit bias & fair housing components
3. **One elective** from: Appraisal, Property Management, Finance, Economics, Legal Aspects, etc.

### Fees
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Pre-license courses | $125 - $700 |
| Examination fee | $100 |
| License application | $350 |
| Fingerprinting | $49 + Live Scan fee |
| **TOTAL** | **$635 - $1,210** |

### Exam Details
- 150 questions, 3 hours 15 minutes
- 70% passing score (105/150 correct)
- Pass rate: ~50%
- Locations: Fresno, La Palma, Oakland, Sacramento, San Diego

## Broker License

### Requirements
- Age: 18+ years
- Experience: 2 years full-time salesperson OR equivalent experience OR real estate degree
- Education: 360 hours (8 courses × 45 hours each)
- Exam: 200 questions, 75% to pass

### Required Courses (8 total)
**Mandatory (5):**
1. Real Estate Practice (with implicit bias component)
2. Legal Aspects of Real Estate
3. Real Estate Finance
4. Real Estate Appraisal
5. Real Estate Economics OR Accounting

**Electives (3):** From approved list

### Fees
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Pre-license courses | $800 - $1,200 |
| Examination fee | $150 |
| License application | $450 |
| Fingerprinting | $49 + Live Scan fee |
| **TOTAL** | **$1,450 - $1,850** |

### Exam Details
- 200 questions, 4 hours
- 75% passing score (150/200 correct)
- Pass rate: ~40%

## License Renewal

### Timeline
- License valid: 4 years
- Late renewal: Up to 2 years after expiration

### Renewal Fees
| License | On-Time | Late (150%) |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Salesperson | $350 | $525 |
| Broker | $450 | $675 |

### Continuing Education (45 hours)

**First Renewal Requirements:**
- Ethics (3 hours)
- Agency (3 hours)
- Trust Fund Handling (3 hours)
- Risk Management (3 hours)
- Fair Housing with interactive role-play (3 hours)
- Implicit Bias Training (2 hours)
- Consumer Protection courses (18 hours minimum)
- Remaining hours: Consumer service or protection courses

**Broker additional:** Management and Supervision (3 hours)

**Subsequent Renewals:**
- 9-hour survey course covering all 7 subjects, OR
- Individual courses covering each mandatory subject
- Plus 18 hours consumer protection

## 2025-2026 Legislative Changes

### SB 1495 - Implicit Bias & Fair Housing (Effective Jan 1, 2024)
- Real Estate Practice course must include:
  - Implicit bias education (impact, historical context, actionable steps)
  - Fair housing component with interactive role-play as consumer AND agent
- Applies to all applications received on/after Jan 1, 2024

### 2026 New Requirements

**Digitally Altered Photos Disclosure (Jan 1, 2026)**
- Must disclose if listing photos are AI-generated or materially altered
- Must provide original unedited photo

**Transit-Oriented Housing (Mid-2026)**
- Limits local zoning restrictions near major transit

**FinCEN Reporting (March 1, 2026)**
- Federal reporting for all-cash residential transactions involving LLCs/trusts

## Processing Times (as of Jan 2026)
- Currently processing mid-to-late December 2025 applications
- Submit well in advance of expiration
