**Research Date:** 2026-01-18
**Source:** Perplexity Deep Research with 60 citations

## License Classifications

### Class A - General Engineering
- Infrastructure, civil engineering projects
- Highways, bridges, dams, pipelines, etc.

### Class B - General Building
- Most common classification
- Structures requiring 2+ unrelated trades
- Cannot do C-16 Fire Protection or C-57 Well Drilling without specialty

### Class B-2 - Residential Remodeling (New as of 2021)
- Existing residential wood frame structures
- Requires 3+ unrelated trades per contract
- Cannot do structural changes, C-16, C-22, C-57

## Requirements

### Experience
- **4 years** at journey level in last 10 years
- Can substitute up to 3 years with education
- Must be verified by qualified person

### Examinations
1. **Law & Business Exam** (all applicants)
   - 7 content areas
   - Closed book, multiple choice
2. **Trade Exam** (classification-specific)

### Bonds
- **Contractor Bond:** $25,000 required
- **Qualifier Bond:** $25,000 (for RME/RMO)
- Cost: ~$100-$500/year depending on credit

### Workers' Comp
- Required unless exempt (no employees)
- C-8, C-20, C-22, C-39, C-61/D-49 ALWAYS required

## 2025-2026 Legislative Changes

### AB 2622 - Handyperson Threshold (Jan 1, 2025)
- **Increased from $500 to $1,000** for unlicensed work
- Still requires license if permit needed or workers hired

### AB 1002 - Wage Enforcement (2026)
- AG and CSLB can jointly act on wage violations
- License suspension/revocation for non-payment

### AB 1327 - Home Improvement Contracts (2026)
- Must include email address
- Allow cancellation via email
- Include phone number for Notice of Cancellation

### SB 291 - Workers' Comp Penalties (2026)
- Increased penalties for violations
- New verification process for exemptions

### SB 456 - Muralist Exemption (2026)
- Muralists exempt from licensing
- Must be unique fine art, hand-painted
- Does not apply to painted signs

### SB 517 - Subcontractor Disclosure (2026)
- Must disclose subcontractor info upon request
- Name, contact, license number, classification

### SB 779 - Increased Civil Penalties (July 1, 2026)
- Unlicensed activity: minimum $1,500
- Other violations: $500-$1,500 minimum
- CPI adjustments every 5 years
