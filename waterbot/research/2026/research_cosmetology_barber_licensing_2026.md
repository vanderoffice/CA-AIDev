# California Cosmetology & Barber Licensing Requirements (2025-2026)

**Research Date:** 2026-01-18
**Source:** Perplexity Deep Research with 60 citations

## Training Hour Requirements

| License Type | Hours | Key Curriculum |
|--------------|-------|----------------|
| **Cosmetologist** | 1,000 | Hair, skin, nails, chemicals |
| **Barber** | 1,000 | Haircutting, shaving, beard trimming |
| **Esthetician** | 600 | Skin care, facials |
| **Electrologist** | 600 | Permanent hair removal |
| **Hairstylist** | 600 | Hair styling (no chemicals) |
| **Manicurist** | 400 | Nail care, pedicures |

*Note: Hours reduced from 1,500-1,600 to 1,000 effective January 1, 2022 (SB 803)*

## Examination Format (2025-2026)

**Written exam only** - practical exams eliminated January 1, 2022

| License | Questions | Time | Pass Score |
|---------|-----------|------|------------|
| Cosmetologist | 100 + 10 pretest | 120 min | 70% |
| Barber | 85 + 10 pretest | 120 min | 70% |
| Esthetician | 75 + 10 pretest | 90 min | 70% |
| Hairstylist | 50 + 10 pretest | 90 min | 70% |
| Manicurist | 60 + 10 pretest | 90 min | 70% |
| Electrologist | 50 + 10 pretest | 90 min | 70% |

**Languages:** English, Korean, Spanish, Vietnamese, Simplified Chinese

## Eligibility Requirements

- Age: 17+ years
- Education: 10th grade completion or equivalent
- No license denial under B&P Code Section 480
- Complete approved training program OR apprenticeship

## Recent Changes (2025-2026)

### Hairstylist Fee Update (SB 1451, Oct 1, 2025)
- Application/exam fee increased to $75 (was $50)
- Aligns with other license types

### Interpreter Regulations (Jan 1, 2026)
- Formalized procedures for exam interpreter use
- For candidates with limited English proficiency

### Pre-Apprentice Training (July 1, 2025)
- Board now administers online course directly
- Previously offered by program sponsors
- Automatic completion reporting to Board

## Continuing Education

**CALIFORNIA DOES NOT REQUIRE CONTINUING EDUCATION**
- No CE credits needed for license renewal
- Optional courses available

## Processing Times

- Online applications: ~3 weeks
- Mail applications: ~8 weeks
- With fingerprinting: up to 12 weeks

## Crossover Programs

- **Cosmetologist → Barber:** 200 hours crossover course
- **Barber → Cosmetologist:** Crossover course at approved school
- **Out-of-state:** 100 hours credit per 3 months licensed experience
