### amendment-process.md
- Amendment triggers (new science, legal changes, TMDLs)
- Triennial review requirement (CWA §303(c), WC §13240)
- Seven-step process with timeline:
  1. Project proposal/request
  2. CEQA scoping (certified regulatory program)
  3. Information gathering and draft development
  4. Peer review
  5. Public review (45-day comment period)
  6. Regional Board hearing and adoption
  7. State Board → OAL → EPA approval
- Typical timeline: 12-24+ months
- Tribal consultation requirements
- Water Code Section 13241 factors
