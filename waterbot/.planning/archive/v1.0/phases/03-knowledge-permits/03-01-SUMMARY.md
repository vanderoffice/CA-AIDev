### Construction General Permit (CGP)
- **Current order:** WQ 2022-0057-DWQ (effective Sept 1, 2023)
- **Threshold:** ≥1 acre land disturbance
- **Fee structure:** $511 base + $54/acre (max $11,223)
- **Risk levels:** 3-tier system based on sediment/receiving water risk
- **2022 changes:** Post-construction plans required with NOI, increased QSD/QSP requirements
- **TMDL requirements:** NAL vs NEL distinction explained

### Industrial General Permit (IGP)
- **Current order:** 2014-0057-DWQ (administratively continued)
- **Applicability:** SIC code-based
- **Fee:** $1,738 flat annual (or $150 for No Exposure Certification)
- **Sampling:** 4 samples/year minimum (TSS, O&G, pH + SIC-specific)
- **Alternative:** No Exposure Certification option
