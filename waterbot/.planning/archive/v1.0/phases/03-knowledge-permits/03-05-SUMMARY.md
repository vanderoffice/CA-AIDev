## Files Created

### Task 1: Water Rights Framework
**File:** `knowledge/03-permits/water-rights/water-rights-overview.md`

Documented California's water rights system including:
- **5 types of water rights:** Riparian, post-1914 appropriative, pre-1914 appropriative, pueblo, prescriptive
- **Permit process:** Application requirements, CEQA review, public notice/protest, permit conditions, license conversion
- **Fees and timelines:** FY 2024-2025 fee schedule, 5-7 year typical timeline
- **Related programs:** Transfers, temporary permits, registration program, SGMA integration
- **Legal references:** Water Code, Title 23 CCR, Constitution Article X §2, SB 389 (2023)

### Task 2: Habitat Restoration Permits
**File:** `knowledge/03-permits/habitat-restoration/restoration-overview.md`

Documented restoration permit pathways including:
- **SRGO (Order WQ 2022-0048-DWQ):** 10 eligible project types, 30-60 day timeline, application process
- **Small Habitat Restoration Order:** ≤5 acres or ≤500 LF, CEQA Cat Ex 15333
- **Accelerated pathways:** SERP (extended to 2030), RMP (2025 pilot), HREA §1652/1653, FRGP
- **Multi-agency coordination:** CDFW §1600, Army Corps §404/§408, NMFS/USFWS programmatic BOs
- **Fee comparison:** $4,212 application vs. up to $365,465 standard
