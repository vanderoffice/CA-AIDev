## Key Facts Captured

- CEQAnet has full text documents since March 2019
- Negative Declaration: 20 days review (30 recommended)
- EIR: 45 days minimum review
- 401 certification pre-filing meeting: 30 days before application
- Water Code 13389 exemption applies ONLY to NPDES WDRs (not all WDRs)
- Certified regulatory programs: Basin planning, instream beneficial use protection
- To request other comments: commentletters@waterboards.ca.gov
