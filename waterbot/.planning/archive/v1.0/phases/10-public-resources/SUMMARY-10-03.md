## Key Facts Captured

- Bagley-Keene requires 1 week notice (10 days if mailed)
- CEQA comment periods: 45 days (may be 30 in exceptional circumstances)
- Water quality standards: 45+ days before hearing
- Designated parties have cross-examination rights; interested persons do not
- Ex parte prohibition applies to adjudicative proceedings only
- Ombudsperson available: contact@waterboards.ca.gov / (916) 341-5254
- Language services available in 6 languages
