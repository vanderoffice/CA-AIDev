# PLAN-07-01: State Water Resources Control Board - Overview

## Objective
Create foundational knowledge files about the California State Water Resources Control Board (State Water Board), covering its structure, authority, and mission.

## Deliverables

### File 1: state-water-board/swrcb-overview.md
**Purpose:** Comprehensive overview of the State Water Board
**Content:**
- What is the State Water Board
- Creation history (Porter-Cologne Act 1969)
- Mission and vision
- Relationship to CalEPA
- Board membership structure (5 members, appointment process)
- Key responsibilities (water quality, water rights, drinking water)

### File 2: state-water-board/legal-authority.md
**Purpose:** Legal framework and statutory authority
**Content:**
- Porter-Cologne Water Quality Control Act
- Clean Water Act delegation
- Safe Drinking Water Act authority
- Water Code provisions
- Enforcement powers
- Regulatory authority scope

### File 3: state-water-board/organizational-structure.md
**Purpose:** Divisions, offices, and organizational hierarchy
**Content:**
- Division of Water Quality
- Division of Water Rights
- Division of Drinking Water
- Division of Financial Assistance
- Office of Chief Counsel
- Office of Public Participation
- Regional Board oversight role

## Research Sources
- waterboards.ca.gov official site
- Porter-Cologne Act text
- California Water Code
- CalEPA organizational documents

Use Perplexity to verify current organizational structure and recent changes.
