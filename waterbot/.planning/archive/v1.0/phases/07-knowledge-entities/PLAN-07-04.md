# PLAN-07-04: Regional Water Boards - Individual Regions

## Objective
Provide detailed information about each of the nine Regional Water Quality Control Boards, their unique characteristics, and key focus areas.

## Deliverables

### File 1: regional-boards/regions-1-3.md
**Purpose:** North Coast, San Francisco Bay, and Central Coast regions
**Content:**
- **Region 1 (North Coast)**
  - Headquarters: Santa Rosa
  - Key issues: Timber harvest, cannabis, salmon habitat
  - Major water bodies: Klamath, Eel, Russian rivers

- **Region 2 (San Francisco Bay)**
  - Headquarters: Oakland
  - Key issues: Urban runoff, bay contamination, wetlands
  - Major water bodies: SF Bay, tributaries

- **Region 3 (Central Coast)**
  - Headquarters: San Luis Obispo
  - Key issues: Agriculture, nitrates, coastal protection
  - Major water bodies: Salinas, Monterey Bay

### File 2: regional-boards/regions-4-6.md
**Purpose:** Los Angeles, Central Valley, and Lahontan regions
**Content:**
- **Region 4 (Los Angeles)**
  - Headquarters: Los Angeles
  - Key issues: MS4 permits, TMDLs, recycled water
  - Major water bodies: LA River, Santa Monica Bay

- **Region 5 (Central Valley)**
  - Headquarters: Rancho Cordova (Sacramento office)
  - Key issues: Agriculture, CV-SALTS, dairies, ILRP
  - Major water bodies: Sacramento, San Joaquin rivers, Delta
  - Largest region by area

- **Region 6 (Lahontan)**
  - Headquarters: South Lake Tahoe & Victorville
  - Key issues: Lake Tahoe clarity, high desert, Owens Valley
  - Major water bodies: Tahoe, Mono Lake, Owens River

### File 3: regional-boards/regions-7-9.md
**Purpose:** Colorado River, Santa Ana, and San Diego regions
**Content:**
- **Region 7 (Colorado River Basin)**
  - Headquarters: Palm Desert
  - Key issues: Salton Sea, agriculture, desert ecosystems
  - Major water bodies: Colorado River, Salton Sea

- **Region 8 (Santa Ana)**
  - Headquarters: Riverside
  - Key issues: Santa Ana River TMDL, groundwater, perchlorate
  - Major water bodies: Santa Ana River, coastal watersheds

- **Region 9 (San Diego)**
  - Headquarters: San Diego
  - Key issues: Border issues, urban development, coastal
  - Major water bodies: San Diego Bay, Tijuana River
