# PLAN-07-02: State Water Board Programs & Divisions

## Objective
Detail the major programs and divisions within the State Water Board that implement water quality and water rights policies.

## Deliverables

### File 1: state-water-board/dwq-programs.md
**Purpose:** Division of Water Quality programs
**Content:**
- NPDES permitting program
- Stormwater programs (Municipal, Industrial, Construction)
- 401 Water Quality Certification
- Nonpoint Source program
- Underground Storage Tank program
- Irrigated Lands Regulatory Program
- Cannabis cultivation program

### File 2: state-water-board/dwr-programs.md
**Purpose:** Division of Water Rights programs
**Content:**
- Water rights administration
- Appropriative rights process
- Water transfers and changes
- Groundwater adjudication support
- Bay-Delta water rights
- Drought response authorities
- Water availability analysis

### File 3: state-water-board/ddw-dfa-programs.md
**Purpose:** Drinking Water and Financial Assistance programs
**Content:**
- Division of Drinking Water overview
- Public water system oversight
- SAFER program (Safe and Affordable Funding for Equity and Resilience)
- Clean Water State Revolving Fund
- Drinking Water State Revolving Fund
- Small Community Funding programs
- Grants and loans administration
