# PLAN-07-05: EPA Region 9 Relationship

## Objective
Document the relationship between California Water Boards and EPA Region 9, including delegated authorities, oversight, and collaborative programs.

## Deliverables

### File 1: epa-region9/epa-delegation.md
**Purpose:** Clean Water Act delegation and federal-state relationship
**Content:**
- NPDES program delegation to California
- Section 401 certification authority
- Section 404 permit relationship (Corps/EPA/State)
- State assumption of wetlands permitting (pending)
- Performance Partnership Agreements
- EPA oversight and audit authority
- Withdrawal of delegation (conditions)

### File 2: epa-region9/epa-programs.md
**Purpose:** Joint EPA/State programs and initiatives
**Content:**
- Water Quality Standards approval process
- TMDL approval and oversight
- Impaired Waters List review
- Enforcement referrals
- Superfund site coordination
- WaterSense and other voluntary programs
- Environmental justice initiatives
- Climate adaptation coordination

### File 3: epa-region9/federal-requirements.md
**Purpose:** Federal requirements California must meet
**Content:**
- Clean Water Act compliance
- Safe Drinking Water Act requirements
- Reporting requirements (ICIS-NPDES, SDWIS)
- Grant requirements (SRF, 106, 319)
- Water quality criteria updates
- Nutrient criteria development
- PFAS and emerging contaminants
- Environmental review (NEPA/CEQA interface)
