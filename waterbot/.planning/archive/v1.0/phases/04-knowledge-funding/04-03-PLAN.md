# Phase 4.3: Federal Funding Programs

## Objective

Document federal funding sources for California water projects across EPA, USDA, FEMA, and other agencies.

## Research Topics

### EPA Programs
- Water Infrastructure Finance and Innovation Act (WIFIA)
- Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (BIL) allocations
- Environmental justice grants
- Clean Water Act Section 319 (NPS)
- Brownfields grants with water components

### USDA Rural Development
- Water and Waste Disposal Loan & Grant Program
- Emergency Community Water Assistance Grants
- Rural Water Circuit Rider Program
- Special Evaluation Assistance for Rural Communities (SEARCH)
- Eligibility (population thresholds, rural definition)

### FEMA Hazard Mitigation
- Hazard Mitigation Grant Program (HMGP)
- Building Resilient Infrastructure and Communities (BRIC)
- Flood Mitigation Assistance (FMA)
- Pre-Disaster Mitigation (PDM)
- Benefit-cost analysis requirements

### Other Federal Sources
- Bureau of Reclamation (WaterSMART, Title XVI)
- Army Corps of Engineers programs
- HUD Community Development Block Grants
- Economic Development Administration
