# PLAN-07-06: Other Regulatory Agencies

## Objective
Document other state and federal agencies that interact with water quality regulation in California.

## Deliverables

### File 1: other-agencies/state-agencies.md
**Purpose:** California agencies with water-related authority
**Content:**
- **CalEPA** - Parent agency, coordination role
- **Department of Water Resources** - Infrastructure, supply planning
- **Department of Fish and Wildlife** - Species protection, 1600 permits
- **Coastal Commission** - Coastal development permits
- **Department of Pesticide Regulation** - Pesticide water quality
- **Office of Environmental Health Hazard Assessment** - Health standards
- **Delta Stewardship Council** - Delta planning
- **State Lands Commission** - Submerged lands

### File 2: other-agencies/federal-agencies.md
**Purpose:** Federal agencies with California water jurisdiction
**Content:**
- **Army Corps of Engineers** - Section 404 permits, flood control
- **Bureau of Reclamation** - CVP operations, water supply
- **Fish and Wildlife Service** - ESA, wildlife refuges
- **NOAA Fisheries** - Marine/anadromous species
- **Coast Guard** - Oil spill response, vessel discharges
- **USDA** - Agricultural programs, NRCS
- **USGS** - Water monitoring, research
- **Bureau of Land Management** - Federal lands

### File 3: other-agencies/coordination-mechanisms.md
**Purpose:** How agencies coordinate on water issues
**Content:**
- CalEPA umbrella structure
- Interagency coordination committees
- CEQA/NEPA lead agency protocols
- Joint permit applications (404/401)
- Memoranda of Understanding
- Delta coordination (SWRCB/DWR/DFW/Reclamation)
- Emergency response coordination
- Information sharing systems
