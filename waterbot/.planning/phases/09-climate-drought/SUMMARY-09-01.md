## Key Topics Covered

### climate-impacts-overview.md
- Fourth Climate Change Assessment findings
- Temperature projections (4-9°F increase by end of century)
- Precipitation pattern changes (same totals, different distribution)
- "Weather whiplash" phenomenon
- Regional impact variations

### climate-water-supply.md
- Snowpack as natural storage (15M acre-feet annually, 1/3 of supply)
- Projected snowpack decline (>50% by end of century)
- Earlier snowmelt timing shifting runoff by 1+ month
- Groundwater overdraft and subsidence
- State Water Project 23% delivery reduction risk

### climate-regulatory-response.md
- State Water Board climate resolutions (2007, 2017, 2021)
- DWR Climate Action Plan three-phase structure
- State Water Project Adaptation Strategy (2025)
- Interagency coordination (Climate Adaptation Strategy, Water Resilience Portfolio)
- Infrastructure modernization efforts (Delta Conveyance, Flood-MAR)
