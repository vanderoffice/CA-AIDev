## Key Insights

1. **Multiple Complaint Pathways**: Water quality vs water rights complaints use different systems - CalEPA portal for quality, Division of Water Rights for rights issues

2. **CIWQS is Central**: Nearly all enforcement data flows through CIWQS public reports - essential starting point for any enforcement research

3. **Transparency is Strong**: California's Public Records Act and open meeting requirements make most enforcement information accessible
