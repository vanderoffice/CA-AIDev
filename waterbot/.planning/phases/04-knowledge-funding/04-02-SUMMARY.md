### irwm-funding.md

- $403M+ awarded through Prop 1 (completed 2023)
- Prop 4: $100M new authorization starting FY 2025-26

- CWSRF: $455.7M grant/PF available
- DWSRF: ~$1B grant/PF available
- FAAST application portal and requirements

- Tribal water security coordination
- DAC/SDAC eligibility definitions
