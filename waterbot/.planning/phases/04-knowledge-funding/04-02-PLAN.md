# Phase 4.2: California Grant Programs

## Objective

Document California state grant programs for water projects, with focus on bond-funded and ongoing programs.

## Research Topics

### Proposition 1 (2014 Water Bond)
- Chapter allocations and remaining funds
- SWRCB-administered programs
- DWR-administered programs (IRWM, flood)
- CDFA water-related programs
- Wildlife Conservation Board allocations

### Integrated Regional Water Management (IRWM)
- Program structure and regions
- Implementation grant process
- Disadvantaged community involvement
- Planning grants vs implementation grants
- Regional coordination requirements

### SWRCB Grant Programs
- Water Recycling Funding Program (WRFP)
- Small Community Grant Fund
- Groundwater Grant Program
- Per- and Polyfluoroalkyl Substances (PFAS) funding
- Water Quality, Supply, and Infrastructure Improvement (Prop 68)

### Emergency and Drought Programs
- Drought relief funding
- Emergency drinking water grants
- Interim water supplies program
