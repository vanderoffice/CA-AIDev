## Key Content Covered

### CWSRF (cwsrf-overview.md)
- Program structure and administration
- Eligible projects: wastewater, stormwater, NPS, emerging contaminants
- Financing terms: 2.2% current rate, 30-year terms
- Priority scoring system (20-point scale, 13-point cutoff)
- BIL principal forgiveness requirements (49% general, 100% emerging contaminants)
- Water Recycling Funding Program integration

### DWSRF (dwsrf-overview.md)
- Six eligible project categories
- Interest rate structure (1.9% for 2025, 0% for SDACs)
- Grant/principal forgiveness maximums ($80K-$120K per connection)
- Priority categories A-F with health risk ranking
- SAFER program integration and achievements
- Consolidation incentives for receiving systems
- Lead service line replacement funding
- Set-asides for technical assistance

### Small Community Funding (srf-small-communities.md)
- DAC/SDAC classification system with MHI thresholds
- Population thresholds (10,000/3,300 connections)
- Water affordability benchmarks (1.5%-2.5% of MHI)
- Technical assistance programs (Prop 1, SADW Fund)
- Direct O&M funding for high-burden systems
- Administrator funding for distressed systems
- Consolidation pathways (physical, managerial, partnerships)
- Step-by-step guide for accessing SRF funding
