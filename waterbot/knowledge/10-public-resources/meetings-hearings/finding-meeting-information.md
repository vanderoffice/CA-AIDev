## CalEPA Building Access

### Directions

**From Bay Area:** I-80 East → I-80 Business (Capital City Freeway) → I-5 North

**From Central/Southern California:** I-5 North or US-99 to I-5 North

**From East:** US-50 West to I-5 North, or I-80 West to I-5 South

**Final approach:** J Street exit, east on J to 11th Street, left to I Street

### Building Location

Block bounded by:
- I Street (south, one-way west)
- H Street (north, one-way east)
- 10th Street (west, one-way north)
- 11th Street (east, two-way)

### Security Procedures

1. Sign in at guard station
2. Receive temporary visitor badge
3. Security contacts staff for escort if needed
4. Surrender badge upon departure

### Parking

- Metered parking (1, 2, and 10-hour meters)
- Accept quarters or special debit cards
- Closest lot: 10th and I Street
- Multiple lots in downtown Sacramento

### Transportation Options

**From Sacramento Airport:**
- YoloBus: Departures every hour, ~18 minutes to 9th and K Street
- SuperShuttle: 24-hour advance notice required
- Vehicle rental

**Amtrak:**
- Sacramento train station: 401 I Street (6 blocks from CalEPA)
- Capitol Corridor, San Joaquin, Coast Starlight, California Zephyr routes

**Light Rail:**
- Regional Transit serves train station every 15 minutes

## Remote Participation

### Hybrid Meeting Format

Since April 5, 2022, meetings offer both in-person and remote options.

### Viewing Only

**No active participation needed:**
- YouTube: [youtube.com/user/BoardWebSupport](https://www.youtube.com/user/BoardWebSupport)
- CalEPA Webcast: [video.calepa.ca.gov](https://video.calepa.ca.gov)
- Closed captioning available

**Note:** Webcast may have slight delay from live meeting

### Active Remote Participation

**Zoom:**
- Meeting ID and password posted day before meeting
- No Zoom account required
- Works in web browser (no software install needed)

**Tips:**
- Complete virtual speaker card to get password
- Join from quiet environment
- Test connection before meeting starts
- Join a few minutes early

### Telephone Participation

- California phone numbers provided
- Dial in with meeting ID
- Press *6 to unmute when called
- Press *9 to raise hand
