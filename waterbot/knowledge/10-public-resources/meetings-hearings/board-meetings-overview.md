# Water Board Meetings Overview

## State Water Resources Control Board

### Meeting Schedule

The State Water Board conducts regular board meetings throughout the year, typically twice monthly during specified periods, with additional workshops.

### Location

**CalEPA Headquarters Building**
- 1001 I Street, Sacramento, CA 95814
- Secure facility with visitor check-in required

### Meeting Calendar

Access the full schedule at: [Board Calendar](https://www.waterboards.ca.gov/board_info/calendar/)

Information includes:
- Meeting dates
- Topics and notices
- Agendas (English and Spanish)
- Video recordings

## Types of Board Meetings

### Regular Board Meetings

Standard business meetings for:
- Making decisions on agenda items
- Voting on permits, orders, and policies
- Hearing public comments
- Staff presentations

**Procedures governed by:** California Code of Regulations Title 23

### Workshops

Specialized meetings for:
- Policy discussions without formal action
- Educational presentations
- Information gathering
- Preliminary deliberations on significant matters

### Public Hearings

Formal quasi-judicial proceedings for:
- Contested permits and orders
- Water rights matters
- Enforcement actions
- Basin plan amendments

**Characteristics:**
- Evidence under oath
- Stenographic recording
- Cross-examination rights (for parties)
- Formal administrative record

## Board Members

### State Water Board

- 5 full-time salaried members
- Different specialty positions
- 4-year terms
- Appointed by Governor, confirmed by Senate

### Regional Boards

- 9 part-time members per board
- 4-year terms
- Appointed by Governor, confirmed by Senate
