- Portal: https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/

## Common Fees (2025)

| Entity Type | Filing Fee | Processing Time |
|-------------|-----------|-----------------|
| LLC | $70 | 1-3 business days (online) |
| Corporation | $100 | 1-3 business days (online) |
| LP/LLP | $70 | 1-3 business days (online) |
| Sole Proprietor | $0 (no state filing) | N/A |
| DBA/FBN | Varies by county | 1-4 weeks |

## Decision Factors

When helping users choose an entity type, consider:
1. **Liability protection** - Personal asset protection needs
2. **Tax treatment** - Pass-through vs corporate taxation
3. **Ownership structure** - Number of owners, investors
4. **Ongoing compliance** - Annual filings, fees
5. **Formality** - Meeting minutes, corporate formalities
