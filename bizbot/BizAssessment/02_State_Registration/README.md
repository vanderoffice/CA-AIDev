## Key Agencies

### Franchise Tax Board (FTB)
- Portal: https://www.ftb.ca.gov/
- Phone: 1-800-852-5711
- Purpose: Business income taxes, $800 minimum franchise tax

### Employment Development Department (EDD)
- Portal: https://eddservices.edd.ca.gov/
- Phone: 1-888-745-3886
- Purpose: Employer registration, payroll taxes (UI, ETT, SDI, PIT)

### California Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA)
- Portal: https://cdtfa.ca.gov/
- Phone: 1-800-400-7115
- Purpose: Seller's permits, sales/use tax, excise taxes

## Registration Sequence

```
1. Entity Formation (SOS) → Get entity number
2. FTB Registration → Get CA tax ID
3. EDD Registration (if hiring) → Get employer account
4. CDTFA Registration (if selling goods) → Get seller's permit
```

## Key Deadlines

| Obligation | Due Date | Penalty |
|------------|----------|---------|
| $800 Minimum Tax | 15th day of 4th month | 5% + interest |
| Statement of Info (LLC) | Within 90 days, then biennial | $250 |
| Statement of Info (Corp) | Within 90 days, then annual | $250 |
| Quarterly Payroll Taxes | Last day of month following quarter | Varies |
