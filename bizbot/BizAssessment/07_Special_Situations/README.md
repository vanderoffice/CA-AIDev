## Certification Programs

### California Small Business (SB) Certification
- **Agency:** DGS Office of Small Business & DVBE Services
- **Benefits:** State contracting preferences (5%)
- **Requirements:** <$15M revenue, <100 employees, CA headquartered
- **URL:** https://caleprocure.ca.gov/

### Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise (DVBE)
- **Agency:** DGS
- **Benefits:** 3% state contract participation goal
- **Requirements:** 51% owned by service-disabled veteran
- **URL:** https://caleprocure.ca.gov/

### Minority Business Enterprise (MBE)
- **Agency:** Various certifying bodies
- **Programs:** CPUC Supplier Diversity, Local agency programs

### Women Business Enterprise (WBE)
- **Agency:** Various certifying bodies
- **Programs:** Federal, state, local procurement preferences

## Federal Programs

### SBA 8(a) Business Development
- For socially/economically disadvantaged businesses
- 9-year program with government contracting preferences

### HUBZone
- Historically Underutilized Business Zone
- Federal contracting preferences for HUBZone-located businesses

### Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB)
- Federal contracting preferences
- Self-certification + verification

## Special Circumstances

### Home-Based Businesses
- See: `03_Local_Licensing/Home_Based_Business.md`
- Zoning restrictions vary by jurisdiction
- Often limited by traffic, signage, employees

### Online-Only Businesses
- Still need business license where headquartered
- Nexus rules for sales tax collection
- May need seller's permit even without physical location

### Out-of-State Businesses
- Must register as "foreign" entity with SOS
- Subject to CA taxes on CA-source income
- May need to qualify to do business
