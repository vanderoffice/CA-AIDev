## Key Annual Obligations

### Secretary of State Filings

| Entity Type | Filing | Frequency | Fee | Penalty |
|-------------|--------|-----------|-----|---------|
| LLC | Statement of Information | Every 2 years | $20 | $250 |
| Corporation | Statement of Information | Annual | $25 | $250 |
| LP/LLP | Statement of Information | Every 2 years | $20 | $250 |

### Franchise Tax Board

| Entity Type | Minimum Tax | Due Date |
|-------------|-------------|----------|
| LLC | $800 | 15th day of 4th month |
| S-Corp | $800 | 15th day of 3rd month |
| C-Corp | $800 | 15th day of 3rd month |
| LP/LLP | $800 | 15th day of 4th month |

**Note:** First-year LLCs (taxable year < 15 days) may be exempt from first-year fee.

### EDD Quarterly Reports

| Report | Due Date | Penalty |
|--------|----------|---------|
| DE 9 (Quarterly Return) | Last day of month after quarter | 15% + interest |
| DE 9C (Individual Detail) | Last day of month after quarter | 15% + interest |

## Professional License Renewals

Most DCA-regulated licenses renew every 2 years. Check the specific board for:
- Renewal dates
- Continuing education requirements
- Renewal fees
