## Key Agencies

### California Air Resources Board (CARB)
- Portal: https://ww2.arb.ca.gov/
- Statewide emissions standards, truck registration

### State Water Resources Control Board
- Portal: https://www.waterboards.ca.gov/
- Stormwater permits, water quality

### Department of Toxic Substances Control (DTSC)
- Portal: https://dtsc.ca.gov/
- Phone: 800-618-6942
- Hazardous waste permits

## Common Environmental Permits

| Permit | Trigger | Agency |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Stormwater (CGP) | Construction >1 acre | Water Board |
| Industrial Stormwater (IGP) | Industrial activity | Water Board |
| Authority to Construct | Equipment with emissions | Local Air District |
| Hazardous Waste Generator | Generate hazmat | DTSC |
| UST Permit | Underground storage tanks | Local CUPA |
