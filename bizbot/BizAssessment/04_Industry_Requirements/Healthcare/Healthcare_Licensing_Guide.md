## Medical Board of California (MBC)

**Agency:** Medical Board of California
**URL:** https://www.mbc.ca.gov
**Phone:** (800) 633-2322

### Physician & Surgeon License Requirements

**Three Core Requirements:**

1. **Education:** Graduate from approved medical school (US, Canadian, or international)
2. **Postgraduate Training:**
   - US/Canadian graduates: 12 months minimum
   - International graduates: 24 months minimum
3. **Examinations:** Pass all required exams (USMLE or equivalent)

### Examination Pathways

**USMLE Pathway (Most Common):**
| Step | Content | Requirement |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Step 1 | Basic Sciences | Pass |
| Step 2 CK | Clinical Knowledge | Pass |
| Step 3 | Clinical Medicine | Pass (max 4 attempts for P&S License) |

**Alternative Pathways:**
- COMLEX-USA (for DO graduates)
- LMCC (Canadian)
- NBME/FLEX (historical)

**Exam Score Validity:** 10 years from passing date

### International Medical Graduates (IMGs)

**Additional Requirements:**
- ECFMG Certification (mandatory)
- 24 months postgraduate training (vs 12 for US grads)
- **2025 Update:** Canadian graduates (degrees on/after 7/1/2025) now require ECFMG certification

### Application Process

**Submit via:** BreEZe online system (https://www.breeze.ca.gov)

**Required Documents:**
- Application + fees
- Live Scan fingerprints
- ECFMG certification (if applicable)
- Official transcripts from medical school
- Postgraduate training verification (Form PTA-PTB)
- Examination scores (submitted directly by testing organization)

**Processing Time:** ~18 days (initial review as of December 2025)

### Costs

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| Application Processing | $625 |
| Initial License | $863 |
| **Total Initial** | **$1,488** |
| Biennial Renewal | $1,206 |
| CURES Fee (included) | $30/year |
| Thompson Corps Fee (included) | $25/year |
| Delinquent Fee | $86.30 |
| Late Penalty (90+ days) | 50% of renewal |

### Postgraduate Training License (PTL)

**For residents in CA ACGME programs:**
- Required within 180 days of program start
- Valid for 36 months
- Requires Step 1 and 2CK only (Step 3 not required)
- Not renewable - must obtain P&S License before expiration

### License Renewal

- **Frequency:** Every 2 years
- **No grace period** - illegal to practice with expired license
- **Notifications:** Email reminders start 180 days before expiration

### Expedited/Priority Review

**Eligible Applicants:**
- Honorably discharged military veterans
- Physicians accepting positions in underserved areas
- Providers of abortion services
- Military spouses stationed in CA
- Refugees, asylees, SIV holders

## Board of Registered Nursing (BRN)

**Agency:** Board of Registered Nursing
**URL:** https://www.rn.ca.gov
**Phone:** (916) 322-3350

### Registered Nurse License - By Examination

**Requirements:**
- Complete BRN-approved prelicensure nursing program
- Pass NCLEX-RN examination

**Application Fees:**
| Applicant Type | Fee |
|----------------|-----|
| California Graduate | $300 |
| Out-of-State Graduate | $350 |
| International Graduate | $750 |
| Repeat Examination | $250 |

### Registered Nurse License - By Endorsement

**Requirements:**
- Current, active RN license in another state/territory/Canada
- Completed education meeting CA requirements
- Passed NCLEX-RN or SBTPE

**Important:** Canadian Comprehensive Exam is NOT acceptable

**2023 Streamlining (CCR §1410.5):**
- Lab component waiver for anatomy/physiology/microbiology
- Available if 2+ years practice in good standing in another state

### Nurse Practitioner (NP) Certification

**Requirements:**
- Active California RN license
- Graduate from NP academic program
- National certification (if not CA-approved program)

**Furnishing Number (NPF):**
- Required to prescribe medications
- Can apply simultaneously with NP application
- Schedule II requires additional pharmacology verification

### 2025 NP Continuing Education Changes

**NEW REQUIREMENT (Effective 1/1/2025):**
- NPs with >25% patients age 65+
- Must complete 6 of 30 CE hours in gerontology/dementia care

### Other Advanced Practice Certificates

| Certificate | Requirements |
|-------------|--------------|
| Clinical Nurse Specialist | RN license + CNS program + certification |
| Nurse Anesthetist | RN license + CRNA program + certification |
| Nurse-Midwife | RN license + CNM program + certification |

### Continuing Education

**Requirement:** 30 contact hours every 2 years

**Topics Must Be:**
- Relevant to nursing practice
- Above entry-level knowledge
- From BRN-recognized providers

**New Requirement (1/1/2023):**
- First renewal: 1 hour implicit bias course

### License Renewal

| Status | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| Timely Renewal | $190 |
| Delinquent Renewal | $280 |
| CURES Fee (2025) | $30/biennium |

**2025 Update:** Public Health Nurse certificates no longer require separate renewal (AB 2471)

## Board of Pharmacy

**Agency:** California State Board of Pharmacy
**URL:** https://www.pharmacy.ca.gov
**Phone:** (916) 518-3100

### Pharmacist License

**Requirements:**
- Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) from accredited school
- Pass NAPLEX examination
- Pass California Pharmacist Jurisprudence Exam (CPJE)

**Continuing Education:**
- 30 hours every 2 years
- 2 hours must be Board-provided Law & Ethics course (effective 7/29/2024)

### Costs

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| Pharmacist Renewal | $450 |
| CURES Fee | $30 |
| **Total Active Renewal** | **$480** |
| Delinquent Total | $630 |

### Pharmacy Technician License

**2025 Fee Reductions:**
| Fee | Previous | 2025 |
|-----|----------|------|
| Application | $195 | $120 |
| Biennial Renewal | $180 | $150 |
| Penalty Fee | $97.50 | $75 |

**Pathways to Licensure:**
- PTCB national certification
- Board-approved training program
- ASHP-accredited program
- ACPE-accredited pharmacy school graduate
- US military pharmacy technician training

### Intern Pharmacist

- Required for supervised practice experience
- Processing time: ~45 days
- License verification available online

### 2026 Nonresident Pharmacy Changes (AB 1503)

**Effective July 1, 2026:**
- Nonresident pharmacies dispensing to CA must designate CA-licensed Pharmacist-in-Charge
- 90-day notification requirement for PIC changes
- Board may require inspections every 4 years

## Dental Board of California (DBC)

**Agency:** Dental Board of California
**URL:** https://www.dbc.ca.gov
**Phone:** (916) 263-2300

### Dentist License Pathways

| Pathway | Requirements |
|---------|--------------|
| WREB | Graduate approved school + WREB exam + law/ethics exam |
| ADEX | Graduate approved school + ADEX exam + law/ethics exam |
| Credential | Active license in another state + verification |
| Residency | 12-month GPR or AEGD completion |

**Foreign-Trained Dentists:**
- Must complete 2-year program at US dental school
- Obtain DMD/DDS degree
- Then apply via ADEX or Residency pathway

### Registered Dental Hygienist (RDH)

**Requirements:**
- Graduate Board-approved dental hygiene program
- Pass WREB dental hygiene clinical exam
- Complete local anesthesia, soft tissue curettage, nitrous oxide courses
- Pass national written exam
- Pass RDH law and ethics exam

### Registered Dental Assistant (RDA)

**Application Fee:** $120

**Pathways:**
- Board-approved RDA program
- 15 months + 1,280 hours work experience with courses
- Certified Dental Assistant (DANB)
- Alternative dental assisting program
- Preceptorship
- US military training

### 2025 Dental Board Updates

**New Infection Control Requirement (1/1/2025):**
- Unlicensed dental assistants must complete 8-hour infection control course
- Before performing procedures with blood/saliva exposure
- Employer responsible for compliance

**RDAEF Exam Blackout:**
- November 14, 2025 - July 31, 2026
- Exam unavailable during maintenance period

**BreEZe Service Fee (1/2/2025):**
- 2.3% credit card fee on all online transactions

### Dentist Renewal

| Status | Fee | CE Required |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| Active | $680 | 50 hours/2yr |
| Inactive | $650 | None |
| Reduced (Active) | $355 | 30 hours/2yr |

**Mandatory CE Courses:**
- Basic Life Support (BLS)
- California Infection Control (max 2 units)
- California Dental Practice Act

### Speech-Language Pathology & Audiology Board

**URL:** https://www.speechandhearing.ca.gov

**Speech-Language Pathologist Requirements:**
- Master's degree in SLP from accredited institution
- 300 hours supervised clinical practicum (3 settings)
- 36 weeks full-time (or 72 part-time) RPE with temp license
- Praxis exam score ≥162

**Audiologist Requirements:**
- Doctoral degree in audiology (effective 1/1/2008)
- 300 hours supervised clinical practicum (3 settings)
- Final year externship
- Praxis 5343 (≥162) or 5342 (≥170)

### Respiratory Care Board

**URL:** https://www.rcb.ca.gov

**Requirements:**
- CoARC-accredited respiratory care program
- Minimum associate degree
- RCB-approved Law and Professional Ethics course (3 hours)

**Military Pathway:**
- 1-year approved program + 2 years full-time military practice

### California Board of Psychology

**URL:** https://www.psychology.ca.gov

**Three-Step Process:**
1. Apply for and pass EPPP ($127 application)
2. Apply for and pass CPLEE ($127)
3. Request initial licensure ($231-236)

**Supervised Experience:**
- Initial: 1,500 hours SPE
- Full: 3,000 hours (1,500 post-doctoral)

**Required Pre-Licensure Courses:**
- Human Sexuality (10 hours)
- Alcohol/Chemical Dependency (15 hours)
- Child Abuse Assessment (7 hours)

**Renewal:** $825/biennium

## Healthcare Facility Licensing (CDPH)

**Agency:** California Department of Public Health
**URL:** https://www.cdph.ca.gov/Programs/CHCQ/LCP

### Facility Types Requiring Licensure

| Facility Type | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| General Acute Care Hospital | Inpatient medical/surgical services |
| Skilled Nursing Facility (SNF) | Extended skilled nursing care |
| Intermediate Care Facility (ICF) | Basic care, skilled nursing supervision |
| Home Health Agency (HHA) | Skilled nursing in patient's residence |
| Ambulatory Surgery Center | Outpatient surgery <24 hours |
| Surgical Clinic | Ambulatory surgical care <24 hours |

### Application Process

**Submit to:** Centralized Applications Branch (CAB)
**NOT to:** Local CDPH District Office

**Processing Time:** Up to 90 days for SNF/ICF applications

### Ambulatory Surgery Centers

**License Exemption:**
- Physician-owned surgical clinics may be exempt under HSC §1206 (Capen Decision)
- Must be owned by Medical Board-licensed physician(s)

**Fees:** No fee for ASC initial certification, CHOW, or ROC applications

### Home Health Agencies

- State license required
- Complete application packet to CAB
- Follow provider instructions and checklist
