## State Requirements

### 1. Seller's Permit (REQUIRED)

**Agency:** California Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA)
**Cost:** FREE
**Timeline:** Same day (online)
**URL:** https://cdtfa.ca.gov/services/

**Who Needs It:** ANY business selling tangible personal property in California

**Includes:**
- Retailers
- Wholesalers
- E-commerce sellers
- Marketplace sellers

**Key Points:**
- No fee to obtain
- Security deposit may be required (covers potential unpaid taxes)
- Separate permit may be required for each location
- NOT the same as a city business license

### 2. Entity Formation

**Agency:** California Secretary of State
**URL:** https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/

| Entity Type | Filing Fee | Processing Time |
|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| Sole Proprietorship | $0 (DBA: $10-80) | N/A |
| LLC | $70 | 5-10 business days |
| Corporation | $100 | 5-10 business days |

### 3. LLC Annual Tax

**Agency:** Franchise Tax Board (FTB)
**URL:** https://www.ftb.ca.gov/file/business/types/limited-liability-company/

| Requirement | Amount | Due Date |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| Annual Minimum Tax | $800 | 15th of 4th month |
| LLC Fee (if revenue >$250K) | $900-$11,790 | 15th of 6th month |

**LLC Fee Schedule:**
| Gross Income | Fee |
|--------------|-----|
| $250,000-$499,999 | $900 |
| $500,000-$999,999 | $2,500 |
| $1,000,000-$4,999,999 | $6,000 |
| $5,000,000+ | $11,790 |

### 4. FTB "Doing Business" Thresholds (2025)

You're "doing business" in California if ANY of these apply:
- CA sales exceed $757,070
- CA property exceeds $75,707
- CA payroll exceeds $75,707

## Local Requirements

### City/County Business License

**Cost:** $50-500+ (varies by jurisdiction)
**Timeline:** 20-45 business days typical

**Important:** Seller's Permit is NOT a substitute for local business license

**Fees may be based on:**
- Flat rate
- Number of employees
- Gross annual revenue
- Industry type

**Example Fees:**
| City | Base Fee |
|------|----------|
| San Diego | Starting at $38 |
| Los Angeles | $150-500+ |
| San Francisco | $91+ |

### Zoning Verification

**CRITICAL:** Verify zoning BEFORE signing lease

**Common Commercial Zones:**
| Zone | Description |
|------|-------------|
| C1 (Limited Commercial) | Small-scale retail, neighborhood shopping |
| C2 (Commercial) | Larger retail, mixed-use |
| C4 (Central Commercial) | Large department stores, urban centers |
| CM (Commercial Manufacturing) | Retail + light industrial |

**San Francisco "Formula Retail" Rules:**
- Businesses with 11+ locations worldwide = "formula retail"
- May require Conditional Use Permit in certain districts
- Prohibited in Hayes-Gough NCD, North Beach NCD, Chinatown Visitor Retail

### Building Permits

**When Required:**
- New construction
- Tenant improvements
- Change of use
- Electrical/plumbing changes

**Timeline:** 2-12 weeks depending on scope

**New 2025 Streamlining (AB 671):**
- Cities must approve/reject small business permits within 20 business days
- If deadline missed, applicants can hire third-party reviewers

### Certificate of Occupancy

**Required:** Before occupying retail space
**Confirms:** Building meets code and is safe for occupancy

## Resale Certificates

### Purpose

Allows purchasing goods for resale WITHOUT paying sales tax at wholesale level

### Requirements for Valid Certificate

Must include:
1. Purchaser name and address
2. Seller's Permit number
3. Description of property to be purchased
4. Statement that property is purchased for resale
5. Date
6. Purchaser signature

**Form:** CDTFA-230 (General Resale Certificate)
**URL:** https://cdtfa.ca.gov/formspubs/cdtfa230.pdf

### Key Rules

- Only for items you intend to resell in your regular course of business
- **Misuse = penalties + potential criminal prosecution**
- Seller can reject suspicious certificates

## Special Retail Categories

### Tobacco Retail

**Additional License:** Cigarette and Tobacco Products Retailer's License
**Agency:** CDTFA
**Cost:** $265/location (annual)
**URL:** https://cdtfa.ca.gov/taxes-and-fees/spctretailers.htm

**Requirements:**
- Separate license per location
- Display prominently
- Maintain records 4 years
- Cannot co-locate with cannabis business

**Electronic Cigarettes (CECET):**
- Additional permit if selling nicotine e-cigarettes
- 12.5% excise tax on retail price
- Quarterly returns required

### Firearms & Ammunition

**Licenses Required:**
1. Federal Firearms License (FFL) - ATF
2. CA DOJ Dealer License
3. Certificate of Eligibility (COE) - DOJ
4. Seller's Permit - CDTFA
5. CFET Certificate - CDTFA
6. Local Business License

**CA Firearm & Ammunition Excise Tax (CFET):**
- 11% on retail sales
- Quarterly returns required
- Effective July 1, 2024

**URL:** https://oag.ca.gov/firearms/dealer-vendor

### Alcohol (Off-Sale)

**Agency:** Alcoholic Beverage Control (ABC)
**URL:** https://www.abc.ca.gov/licensing/license-types/

| License Type | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| Type 20 | Off-Sale Beer & Wine |
| Type 21 | Off-Sale General (includes spirits) |

**Timeline:** 6 weeks to several months

**Note:** Both Type 20 and 21 allow minors on premises

### Secondhand Dealers & Pawn Shops

**License From:** Local Police Chief or Sheriff
**Agency:** DOJ Secondhand Dealer and Pawnbroker Unit
**URL:** https://oag.ca.gov/secondhand

**Requirements:**
- Background check required
- Must use CAPSS (California Automated Pawn and Secondhand Dealer System)
- New education requirements effective January 1, 2026 (AB 2231)

## Online Retail / E-Commerce

### Economic Nexus Threshold

**$500,000** in CA sales = must register for sales tax collection

This applies even with NO physical presence in California

### Marketplace Facilitator Rules

If you sell through a marketplace (Amazon, Etsy, etc.):
- **Marketplace collects and remits tax** on your behalf
- You may still need Seller's Permit for direct sales
- Shopify itself is NOT a marketplace (you collect tax)
- Shopify Shop app IS a marketplace (they collect tax)

### Tax Collection

You must collect tax based on **delivery location**, not your business location

## Signage Requirements

### Sign Permits

**Required for:** Most permanent and temporary signs
**Cost:** $50-500+ depending on size/type
**Timeline:** Same day to 2 weeks

**Process:**
1. Review Sign Program or Sign Ordinance
2. Design sign per requirements
3. Get property owner approval
4. Submit permit application
5. Engineering certification (if required)
6. Install per permit
7. Final inspection

**Prerequisites:**
- Valid Business License
- Certificate of Occupancy

### 2025 Updates

New regulations emphasize:
- Accessibility (braille, high-contrast, easy-to-read fonts)
- Environmental sustainability
- Brightness limits for digital signs (especially nighttime)

## ADA Compliance

### Key Requirements

| Area | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Parking | 96" wide spaces, 60" access aisle, close to entrance |
| Aisles | Minimum 36" wide, clear of obstacles |
| Doorways | Minimum 32" clear width |
| Door handles | Easy to operate with minimal force |
| Checkout counters | Maximum 36" height, clear wheelchair space |
| Product placement | 15-48" from ground for easy reach |
| Fitting rooms | 60" diameter clear floor, grab bars, 32" doors |
| Signage | High-contrast, tactile, Braille where needed |
| Ramps | 1:12 slope ratio, wide enough for two people |

### Compliance Resources

- **CASp Inspection:** Certified Access Specialist can review your property
  - Demonstrates good faith
  - May reduce liability in lawsuits
- **CalCAP ADA Financing:** Loans for accessibility improvements
- **Tax Credits:** Federal deductions for barrier removal

**URLs:**
- https://calosba.ca.gov/for-small-businesses-and-non-profits/disability-access-regulations/
- https://www.dgs.ca.gov/DSA/Resources/CASp-Program

## Timeline Overview

```
Week 1-2: Foundation
├── Form LLC/Corp (if applicable)
├── Get EIN
├── Apply for Seller's Permit
└── Verify zoning with city

Week 2-4: Local Approvals
├── Apply for business license
├── Submit building permit (if needed)
└── Apply for sign permit

Week 4-8: Inspections & Final Steps
├── Building inspections (if construction)
├── Fire inspection
├── Certificate of Occupancy
└── ADA review

Week 8+: Operations
├── Business license issued
├── Open for business
└── Begin sales tax collection/filing
```

**Total Timeline:** 4-12 weeks (general retail)
