## Key Regulatory Agencies

### Department of Consumer Affairs (DCA)
- Portal: https://www.dca.ca.gov/
- Phone: (800) 952-5210
- Oversees 40+ professional licensing boards

### Contractors State License Board (CSLB)
- Portal: https://www.cslb.ca.gov/
- Phone: (800) 321-2752
- 43 contractor classifications

### Alcoholic Beverage Control (ABC)
- Portal: https://www.abc.ca.gov/
- Phone: (916) 419-2500
- All alcohol manufacture, distribution, retail

### Department of Cannabis Control (DCC)
- Portal: https://cannabis.ca.gov/
- Phone: (844) 612-2322
- All commercial cannabis activities
