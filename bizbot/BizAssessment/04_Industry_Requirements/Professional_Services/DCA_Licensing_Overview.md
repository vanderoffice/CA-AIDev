## Certified Public Accountants (CPAs)

### Board

**Agency:** California Board of Accountancy (CBA)
**URL:** https://www.dca.ca.gov/cba

### Requirements

**Education (150 semester units total):**
- Baccalaureate degree from accredited institution
- 24 units accounting subjects
- 24 units business-related subjects
- 20 additional units accounting study
- 10 units ethics education

**Experience:** 12 months general accounting experience

**Examination:** Uniform CPA Exam (4 sections)
- Auditing and Attestation (AUD)
- Financial Accounting and Reporting (FAR)
- Regulation (REG)
- One Discipline section (BAR, ISC, or TCP)

### Costs

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| Initial Application | $100 |
| Reapplication | $50 |
| NASBA Exam Fees (per section) | ~$265 |
| Total Exam Fees (4 sections) | ~$1,062 |

### Timeline

- 30-month rolling credit for passing exam sections
- All 4 sections must be passed within 30 months

### Continuing Education

| Requirement | Amount |
|-------------|--------|
| Per 2-year renewal | 80 hours |
| Minimum per year | 20 hours |
| Technical subjects per year | 12 hours minimum |

## Real Estate Professionals

### Agency

**Department:** California Department of Real Estate (DRE)
**URL:** https://www.dre.ca.gov

### Salesperson License

**Requirements:**
- Age 18+
- Complete 3 college-level real estate courses:
  - Real Estate Principles
  - Real Estate Practice
  - One elective (Finance, Appraisal, Law, etc.)

### Broker License

**Requirements:**
- 2 years salesperson experience (or equivalent)
- Complete 8 required courses including:
  - Real Estate Practice (with implicit bias training as of 1/1/2024)
  - Legal Aspects of Real Estate
  - Real Estate Finance
  - Real Estate Appraisal
  - Real Estate Economics or Accounting
  - 3 additional electives

### Costs

| Fee | Salesperson | Broker |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| Examination | $100 | $150 |
| License Application | $350 | $450 |
| Fingerprint Processing | $49 | $49 |
| **Total Initial** | **$499+** | **$649+** |
| Renewal (on-time, 4-year) | $350 | $450 |
| Late Renewal | $525 | $675 |

### Continuing Education

- **45 hours every 4 years**
- Includes 8-hour ethics/fair housing course
- 18 hours consumer protection courses

## Insurance Agents

### Agency

**Department:** California Department of Insurance (CDI)
**URL:** https://www.insurance.ca.gov

### Life Insurance License

**Requirements:**
- Age 18+
- Complete 12 hours approved study (ethics + CA Insurance Code)
- Minimum 1 hour on insurance fraud prevention
- Pass license examination

**2025 Annuity Requirements (SB 263):**
- 8-hour initial annuity training course (before selling annuities)
- 4-hour Annuity Suitability and "Best Interest Standard" course (before first renewal)

### Costs

| Fee | Amount |
|-----|--------|
| Initial License | $188 |
| Renewal | $188 |

### Continuing Education

- **24 hours every 2 years**
- Minimum 3 hours ethics
- 4-hour annuity course required for annuity sellers (2025)

## Attorneys

### Agency

**Organization:** State Bar of California
**URL:** https://www.calbar.ca.gov

### Requirements

1. **Registration** with State Bar as law student or attorney applicant
2. **Education:** JD from ABA-approved or CA-accredited law school
3. **Bar Exam:** 2-day California Bar Examination
4. **MPRE:** Multistate Professional Responsibility Examination
5. **Moral Character:** Background check determination

**Attorney's Examination:** Available for attorneys with 4+ years active license in another state (1-day written exam only)

### Timeline

- Bar Exam: February and July each year
- Results: Several months post-exam

## Engineers

### Board

**Agency:** Board for Professional Engineers, Land Surveyors and Geologists (BPELSG)
**URL:** https://www.bpelsg.ca.gov

### License Categories

**Practice Acts (only licensed may practice):**
- Civil Engineering
- Electrical Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering

**Title Acts (only licensed may use title):**
- Agricultural, Chemical, Control Systems, Fire Protection
- Industrial, Metallurgical, Nuclear, Petroleum, Traffic Engineering

**Subspecialties:**
- Structural Engineering (Civil subspecialty)
- Geotechnical Engineering (Civil subspecialty)

### Civil Engineering Requirements

1. **FE Exam:** Fundamentals of Engineering (NCEES)
2. **PE-Civil Exam:** Principles and Practice of Engineering
3. **State-Specific Exams:**
   - Civil Seismic Principles
   - Civil Engineering Surveying

**Experience:** 4+ years professional engineering experience

**Application:** Online via BPELSG Connect portal

## Architects

### Board

**Agency:** California Architects Board (CAB)
**URL:** https://www.cab.ca.gov

### Requirements

1. **Education/Experience:** 8 years combined training and education
2. **ARE:** NCARB Architect Registration Examination
3. **CSE:** California Supplemental Examination

**2025 Update (effective 10/1/2025):**
- Can now receive testing authorization before documenting all 8 years of experience
- Final license still requires complete 8-year documentation

### Continuing Education

- Required for biennial renewal
- Must retain documentation for 2 years post-renewal

## Healthcare Professionals

### Physicians & Surgeons

**Board:** Medical Board of California
**URL:** https://www.mbc.ca.gov

**Requirements:**
- Graduate from approved medical school (US, Canadian, or international)
- International graduates: ECFMG certification required
- Canadian graduates (after 7/1/2025): ECFMG certification now required
- Postgraduate training:
  - US/Canadian grads: 12 months minimum
  - International grads: 24 months minimum
- Pass required examinations (USMLE or COMLEX-USA)

**180-Day Rule:** Physicians training in CA must obtain license within 180 days of starting program

**Processing Time:** ~32 days (complete applications)

### Registered Nurses

**Board:** Board of Registered Nursing (BRN)
**URL:** https://www.rn.ca.gov

**Requirements:**
- Complete approved nursing program
- Pass NCLEX-RN examination

**2025 Changes:**
- Public health nurse certificate renewal eliminated (1/1/2025)
- NPs with 25%+ patients age 65+: 6 of 30 CE hours must be in gerontology/dementia care
- CURES fee increase: $9 â†’ $15/year (effective 7/1/2025)

**Processing Time:** 10-12 weeks (complete applications)

### Pharmacists

**Board:** California State Board of Pharmacy
**URL:** https://www.pharmacy.ca.gov

**Requirements:**
- Complete pharmacy education program
- Pass MPJE (Multistate Pharmacy Jurisprudence Examination)
- Pass NAPLEX examination

**2026 Update (AB 1503):** Nonresident pharmacies must designate CA-licensed Pharmacist-in-Charge by 7/1/2026

### Dentists

**Board:** Dental Board of California
**URL:** https://www.dbc.ca.gov

**Licensure Pathways:**
- WREB Examination
- ADEX Examination
- Licensure by Credential
- Residency Program Completion

**Foreign Graduates:** Must complete 2 years at US dental college + obtain DMD/DDS

**2025 Update:** Unlicensed dental assistants must complete 8-hour infection control course before performing procedures with blood/saliva exposure

**Processing Time:** 6-8 weeks (complete applications)

## Personal Services

### Barbers & Cosmetologists

**Board:** Board of Barbering and Cosmetology
**URL:** https://www.barbercosmo.ca.gov

**Training Requirements:**

| License | Hours | Key Components |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| Barber | 1,000 | 100 hr sanitation, 200 hr shaving, 700 hr general |
| Cosmetologist | 1,000 | 100 hr sanitation, 200 hr chemical, 400 hr general |
| Esthetician | 600 | 100 hr sanitation, 350 hr skin care |
| Manicurist | 400 | 100 hr sanitation, 150 hr manicure/pedicure |
| Electrologist | 600 | Electrolysis procedures and safety |

**Establishment License:** Required for salon/shop locations

### Tattoo Artists

**Regulation:** County Health Departments (not statewide board)
**Law:** Safe Body Art Act (Health and Safety Code)

**Requirements:**
- Register with county health department
- OSHA-approved Bloodborne Pathogen training
- Hepatitis B vaccination (or medical exemption)
- Government-issued photo ID
- Proof of residency

**Restrictions:**
- NO tattooing minors (even with parental consent)
- Facility plans must be approved before construction

**Example (LA County):**
- Registration: $54/year
- Processing: ~1 week
