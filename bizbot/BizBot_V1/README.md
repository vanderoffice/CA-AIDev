## üìÇ Repository Structure

The repository is organized into core process documentation and assessment resources.

### Core Documentation

These files outline the 4-phase process for business licensing:

- **`AGENTS.md`**: The central agent definition, outlining the mission, core phases, and response protocols.
- **`entity-formation.md`** (Phase 1): Guide to establishing a legal business entity (LLC, Corp, etc.) with the Secretary of State.
- **`state-licensing.md`** (Phase 2): Information on state-level permits like the CDTFA Seller's Permit.
- **`local-licensing.md`** (Phase 3): Details on city/county business licenses and critical zoning clearances.
- **`renewal-compliance.md`** (Phase 4): Ongoing maintenance, renewals, and tax filing requirements.
- **`industry-requirements.md`**: Supplemental specific requirements for various industries.

### Assessment & Research

- **`BizAssessment/`**: A directory containing research and interview transcripts with AI models simulating California State department stakeholders. See the [BizAssessment README](BizAssessment/README.md) for more details.

## üöÄ Getting Started

If you are building or using the agent, follow the **Core Process** sequence:

1.  **Entity Formation**: Establish the business structure.
2.  **State Licensing**: Acquire necessary state permits.
3.  **Local Licensing**: Secure local zoning and business tax certificates.
4.  **Renewal & Compliance**: Maintain good standing.
